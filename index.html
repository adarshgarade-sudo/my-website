Welcome to Digital Farm MRL & AMU Portal

Empowering farmers, veterinarians, and regulators through smart digital technology for monitoring antimicrobial usage and ensuring food safety.

About the Portal

This platform enables real-time monitoring of antimicrobial usage (AMU) and compliance with Maximum Residue Limits (MRL). It integrates with veterinary systems and provides blockchain-based traceability to ensure transparency and trust.
